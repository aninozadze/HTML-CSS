About the Experience

Virtual Mindfulness Experience at Odawara Castle



Join us for a session of mindfulness as we find calm and peacefulness within ourselves. This exclusive experience is held from inside the famous Odawara Castle in Japan and hosted by a Japanese guide to mindfulness.


A main goal of this program is to share the methods and knowledge of mindfulness to increase happiness and peace for yourself and the world around us. Mental health has become even more important in our busier and more digitized lives. Take the time to learn the ancient lessons and tools to take care of yourself mentally and spiritually. This exclusive experience is held in both English and Japanese.






What to Expect


Most of our lives are structured around the people and events around us, the goal of this experience is to find time for yourself. Breathing, introspection, and meditation will be a main focus of our time together with Iwayama Tomomi, the founder of Flying Monk in Odawara, Japan. Past participants have included individuals and even companies looking to find ways to relieve stress and apply the teachings of mindfulness to the daily lives. At the end we encourage open discussion and questions.

What to Get Ready

This is a virtual online experience hosted online. Find comfortable place and clothes to enjoy this online experience. Bring your curiosity and sense of imagination as we guide you through the world of the Mindfulness and Odawara Castle. The host is ready to answer any of your questions.
